Key Design Decisions

3 Tabs

Home (capture flow), Meetings (browse/manage), Settings. Clean and focused.

Linear Capture Flow

Record/Upload → Processing → Transcript → Minutes. Each step pushes onto the navigation stack.

Shared Screens

Minutes, Edit, and Export screens are reused from both Home and Meetings tabs.

8 Unique Screens Total

Dashboard, Recording, Processing, Transcript, Minutes, Edit, Export, Settings. Plus Meetings list.
